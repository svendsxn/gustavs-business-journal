## Tools for Content Creation

**Kling Video 3.0 (via Replicate)** is great for video making. High quality output, easy to run through the Replicate API.

**Replit** is great for making animations if prompted correctly. The key is giving it clear, specific prompts describing the animation you want.

### MODU ESG Green Wavy Background

> make background effect wavy motion graphics, make it clearly visible that background is moving
